The Chinese Students and Scholars Association at The New School has been actively helping the Chinese community to build a sense of belonging in New York City. We dedicated to providing support and resources for Chinese students and scholars and serve as a platform for the interaction of the Chinese community. We promote cultural exchange through organized activities and connections with other organizations. We collaborate with CSSAs at other schools and universities as well.
