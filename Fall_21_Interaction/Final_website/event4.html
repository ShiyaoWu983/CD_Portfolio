Spring Gala
The 15th day of the first month is the first full moon of the year. In ancient times, when there was a strict legislative system, the Lantern Festival allowed people to entertain themselves freely, and it became a "carnival" for people at the beginning of the year.




Fordham CSSA and The New School CSSA are jointly organizing an offline Spring Lantern Party for the Year of the Tiger!


The Spring Lantern Party will not only bring you the traditional atmosphere of your hometown and professional performances but also provide a stage for all the greats. Under the spotlight, all dreamers show their skills, show their potential, and realize their stage dreams.
Fordham CSSA and The New School CSSA
invite you to join us in creating
a meaningful Spring Lantern Party.
Notes for the audition


Online video audition
12/15/2021 EST 24:00


Entries must reflect the ability of the program and performers, but no special preparation for the theme is required All video submissions should be limited to 3 minutes or less in length.

If the overall program is longer than three minutes, you may submit a representative or outstanding clip.

Offline audition
12/18-19/2021 EST

The offline audition requires the performer to present a full show, not a clip of the show.
