Swirl, Scuffle, Sequel: An Episo-demic Saga is a 100% student-led online exhibition for the public good, with the participation of multiple groups from individual international students or their start-up projects or teams from major universities in North America.

In the unpredictable "post-epidemic era", our mission is becoming increasingly difficult: therefore, ULand, together with the New College China Student Association, is now planning to launch an online "cloud" exhibition for "art and design universities and other creative projects around the world". The "Cloud" exhibition will give art and design students a platform to showcase their creations, and at the same time implement the spirit and ethos of the New School, from the perspective of "supporting the new generation of young artists, designers and new talents in the field of culture and creativity in China". We will share the opportunity to participate in this art exhibition and the opportunity to improve their background after their works are selected to every student in the related fields.

An Episo-demic Saga: An Ode to Episodic Infectious Diseases
Episo-demic = Episodic + Epidemic

The art exhibition will be divided into three parts:
                                                                            Swirl, Scuffle, Sequel





Act I - Swirl
At the beginning of every disaster, epidemic and crisis, everyone's life seems to be sucked into a huge whirlpool.

Act II - Scuffle
In the midst of the disasters, epidemics and crises that swept the world, people fought against them as if they were caught in a short but chaotic struggle.

Act III - Sequel
As disasters, epidemics and crises fade away, people embrace calm again. Light years away, each seemingly tragic crisis in fact becomes a star point in the history of mankind and the world, becoming the latest chapter in the sequence called "mega-disaster".
